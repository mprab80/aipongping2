AI PING PONG GAME






Developed By Nithil

Instructions

  1. First keep your laptop screen straight.
  2. Move yorself approximately 3-4 feet away from your webcam
  3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner, a red dot should appear on your right wrist.
  4. Now press the play button, move 3-4 feet away from your laptop
  5. The red paddle is yours, that will move up and down as per the movement of your right wrist.
